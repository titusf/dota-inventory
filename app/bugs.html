Bugs, discussion and feedback

Below is a list of recognised bugs with DotaInventory.com. If you have found a bug that's not on this list, please add it as a comment below. Feel free to discuss any other issues or recommendations you may have with the site also. Thanks!

Unsolved Bugs

  • Sometimes pages may need to be refreshed to work properly.
  • Some alphanumeric characters do not display properly (e.g. in Russian names).
  • Issue with friendslist items where items are saved from previous page.
  • Some items are missing from a user's inventory but the data is not missing when pulled from Steam.

Resolvable Issues

  • Bundles do not display which items they contain. Neither do items that belong to a bundle display this.
  • Page title gets stuck on a user's name.
