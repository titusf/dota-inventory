Browse

DotA 2 has a large and constantly growing collection of cosmetic items. Dota Inventory makes it easy to find cool new items and browse the existing library.

Is Meepo your favourite hero? Or maybe you want to know which Immortal items are out there? There's plenty of items out there so start browsing now!

Trade

Steam Trading is huge thanks to Steam's real-money marketplace and Dota 2's wide range of cosmetic items.

Dota Inventory provides basic trading support. Do you want that Dragonclaw Hook? Visit the item's page and see if anyone's looking to trade it. Alternatively post your own items up for trade.

Start Trading

Merge

Dota Inventory started out as a basic friendslist item browser which let you combine every item owned by one of your Steam friends into a simple list.

Expanding on that, Dota Inventory is now a place to view any player's inventory as well as a combined friendslist inventory.
